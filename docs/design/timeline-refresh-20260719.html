Timeline refresh button

2026-07-19 · Phase 1 session 3 (home timeline infinite scroll + manual refresh). Constraint discovered against app-shell-20260705.html: the bottom-right FAB slot is already reserved for Compose (Phase 2), so a refresh FAB cannot simply take it.

Trade-offs

  • A (stacked above Compose): thumb-zone reach, always visible. Phase 2 brings a two-FAB stack at bottom-right — acceptable if refresh stays a small secondary FAB, but the corner gets busy and covers more card content.
  • B (switcher row, sticky): no overlay over content, natural place also shared by the future timeline switch (this row is where home/local/bubble/fedi live — one control strip). Needs the row sticky to stay pressable mid-scroll; top-of-screen reach is worse one-handed, though refresh after AFK typically happens when picking the phone up, not mid-scroll.
  • C (bottom-left FAB): always reachable and never collides with Compose, but the weak corner for right-handed thumbs and covers card content on the line-start side where text begins.

Decision (2026-07-19)

  • B chosen. The sticky control strip at the top of the column hosts the refresh button at its right end; the timeline switcher tabs will join the same strip in their own story. Reasoning: no FAB collision with the reserved Compose slot, no content overlay, and refresh-after-AFK happens at phone pick-up rather than mid-scroll, so the top-reach penalty is small in practice. Until the switcher story lands, the strip carries only the refresh control (right-aligned).
  • Reaffirmed after a dogfooding challenge (same day). A refined bottom-right FAB (option A revival) was argued on frequency-vs-reach ergonomics and explicitly declined; the single-control-home principle and content-occlusion cost won. Visual spec settled with it: the strip gets a quiet "Home" label at its left edge (text.brand — the future switcher tabs' opening position), and refresh is a 40px ghost icon button at the right end using an inline SVG arrow (never a Unicode glyph — font-dependent, renders as emoji on Android), spinning while a refresh is in flight (static and dimmed under prefers-reduced-motion).
  • Panel enclosure (same day, dogfooding — supersedes two interim treatments). Flush-under-the-header read as a broken second header on desktop (the interim shell header is viewport-wide, the strip column-wide); a detached card-style strip then read as an unrelated card — nothing showed the bar and the timeline belong together (akkoma-fe's flush "ホームタイムライン" bar-on-list was the reference that named the problem). Settled: the timeline is one bordered, rounded panel whose top edge is the bar (Home label + refresh; the switcher tabs slot into this same bar later — this is the app-shell desktop wireframe's own drawing). Containment carries the relationship. On mobile (column = viewport width) the panel drops its border/radius and the bar degrades to the flush full-width strip, which matches the header width there. The bar stays sticky; no overflow clipping on the panel, since that would re-scope the sticky bar away from the viewport.
  • Flush ⟺ uncapped, keyed to one breakpoint. The flush treatment is correct exactly while the column is full-width, so the column's 600px cap and the flush→panel switch are both keyed to the standard md breakpoint (the column is uncapped below md). A custom 600px breakpoint synced to the cap was tried and rejected: the invariant then rests on a numeric match across files, and Panda emits breakpoints in rem while the cap is px, so enlarged root font sizes silently re-open a broken band. Cost accepted: between 600px and md the column runs full width, so body lines exceed the ~600px reading width on that band of viewports.

Notes

  • New statuses prepend above the current reading position (browser scroll anchoring keeps the viewport still), so after pressing refresh the user scrolls up to read — the button itself does not need to sit where the new content appears.
  • Streaming (Phase 4) will revisit this control as an auto/manual toggle; whichever spot is chosen here becomes that toggle's home.
